Project: Student Account Merges

Main About Me Projects UAT Boards Objectives

Project Goals:

Uphold data integrity through merging duplicate records, logging processes as the project goes for transparency.

Ensure ethical use of personal student information, upholding accuracy and student privacy.

Tools Used:

CampusNexus CRM

HubSpot CRM

Description:

During Student Merges, I integrate data from the CampusNexus CRM to merge duplicate student accounts, handling sensitive PII such as contact info and social security numbers. While doing so, I ensure that data governance principles are followed and I adhere to best practices for digital privacy, including adhering to FERPA and HIPAA regulations. This project also includes thorough logging and auditing procedures to ensure transparency and integrity.
