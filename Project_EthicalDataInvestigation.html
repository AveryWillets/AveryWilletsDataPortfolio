Project: Ethical Data Investigation

Main About Me Projects UAT Boards Objectives

Project Goals:

Identify ethical and unethical uses of personal information in investigations.

Maintain and respect digital privacy when conducting analysis and document steps taken to uphold investigative integrity.

Tools Used:

Autopsy

Excel

Description:

In this project, I identify all pieces of potential evidence based on files provided and the facts of the case. I create a chain of custody log sheet for the investigation and ensure that the scope is not exceeded. I also maintain ethical use of personal information by identifying evidence that can and cannot be admissible in court.
